  • Perhaps history's greatest practioner of shock rhetoric is Rabbi Yeshua ben Yusef.

  • "He who is without sin - cast the first stone....render on to Caesar the things of Caesar, and on to God the things that are God's...evil things that are done in secret will be shouted from the rooftops..."
